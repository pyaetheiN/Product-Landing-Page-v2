What is Family Hub™

Samsung’s Family Hub,™ now with Alexa built-in, lets you control your Samsung smart appliances and devices, stream music, share pictures with your family, and so much more, all right from your fridge.

What is a counter-depth?

Counter-depth refrigerators are typically under 30 inches deep. By contrast, traditional refrigerators may stick out half a foot or more from the edge of the kitchen counters.
